The performance of the model can be viewed in graphical form here. As more data is collected the model will become more accurate.

How does the model work?

The machine learning model is trained on the collected data; wind magnitude, swell height, swell period, water temperature, days since last rain, rainfall in the past week and visibility. It can then make predictions for visibility based on the previously mentioned factors.
